Welcome to Steamies

Hey there, young adventurers! Welcome to our magical world! We’re so excited to meet you!
Each of us has a special talent and a story to share. Let’s introduce ourselves, shall we?

Spark

Hello! My name is Spark, and I love exploring how the world works! From the stars in the sky to the plants in your garden, science is all around us. With me, you'll discover fun experiments, learn about animals, space, and cool science facts. Get ready to ask questions and become a super scientist!

    WHAT YOU WILL LEARN:
  • - The scientific method
  • - Basic biology, chemistry, and physics concepts
  • - How to conduct fun experiments

Techno

Hey there, I’m Techno! I’m all about gadgets, robots, and computers. With me, you’ll dive into the exciting world of technology. You’ll learn how to code, build simple robots, and see how technology makes our lives easier. I can't wait to show you how cool tech can be!

    WHAT YOU WILL LEARN:
  • - Basic coding and programming
  • - Introduction to robotics
  • - Understanding how everyday technology works

Engine

Hi, I’m Engine! I’m here to help you build and create amazing things. Whether it’s bridges, towers, or even designing your own inventions, engineering is all about problem-solving and making the world a better place. Together, we'll use our imagination to construct and design awesome projects!

    WHAT YOU WILL LEARN:
  • - Building and design concepts
  • - Simple machines and structures
  • - Problem-solving and creative thinking

Arty

Hi, I’m Arty, the creative one! I love painting, drawing, and using my imagination to make the world colorful. With me, you’ll explore all kinds of art, from painting to crafts and even digital design. Let’s express ourselves and make beautiful things together!

    WHAT YOU WILL LEARN:
  • - Different forms of visual art
  • - Crafting and creativity
  • - Digital art and design basics

Max

Hello, I’m Max, the master of numbers! Math might sound tricky, but with me, it’s all about fun puzzles, patterns, and games. You’ll learn how to solve problems, play with shapes, and understand numbers in exciting ways. Let’s explore the magic of math together!

    WHAT YOU WILL LEARN:
  • - Basic math skills (addition, subtraction, etc.)
  • - Patterns, shapes, and numbers
  • - Problem-solving through math games
